**Centurio: Your Personal Finance Companion**

Take control of your spending with Centurio, the intelligent budget tracker that makes expense logging effortless. Whether you're on the go or reviewing your finances, Centurio adapts to your lifestyle.

**🎤 Voice-Powered Expense Logging**
Simply speak your expenses and let AI do the work. "Twenty-five bucks at McDonald's" - done! No typing, no hassle.

**📸 Smart Receipt Scanning**
Snap a photo of your receipt and watch as Centurio automatically extracts all items, amounts, and categories. Perfect for grocery shopping or dining out.

**📊 Visual Spending Insights**
- 31-day spending heatmap shows your patterns at a glance
- Category breakdowns help you understand where your money goes
- Real-time daily totals keep you on track

**✏️ Full Control**
- Edit any transaction with a tap
- Change categories on the fly
- Delete mistakes instantly
- Create custom categories with minimalist icons

**🔒 Secure & Private**
Your financial data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information with third parties.

**🌙 Beautiful Design**
Minimalist interface with automatic dark mode support. Clean, modern, and distraction-free.
